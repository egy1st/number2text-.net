setCurrency Function

setCurrency function enables you to customize your currency, so you may change default values to yours customization.

default currency used by English language is Dollar , while Euro is default currency for French , German, Italian, Spanish and Portuguese Languages , and is default currency for Arabic Language

Syntax:

Public Sub setCurrency(ByVal oneCurrency As String, ByVal CurrencyPlural As String, ByVal oneCurrencyUnit As String, ByVal CurrencyUnitPlural As String)

this function takes 4 Parameters:
first parameter oneCurrency  is your customized currency in single
secondt parameter CurrencyPlural is your customized currency in plural

third parameter oneCurrencyUnit is your customized coins unit in single

fourth parameter CurrencyUnitPlural is your customized coins in plural

Example:

SetCurrency("dollar","dollars","cent","cent")

when you use TtranslateNumber function , result may be something like that
four hundreds and twenty one dollars and twenty three cent
