-1 mainasu ichi (minus one)マイナス一
1+2=3 ichi tasu ni wa san
('wa' is 'ha' in Hiragana)		 一足す二は三
3-2=1 san hiku ni wa ichi
('wa' is 'ha' in Hiragana)		 三引く二は一
2*3=6 ni kakeru san wa roku
('wa' is 'ha' in Hiragana)		 二掛ける三は六
6/3=2 roku waru san wa ni
('wa' is 'ha' in Hiragana)		 六割る三は二
7/3=2, 1 nana waru san wa ni, amari ichi
('wa' is 'ha' in Hiragana)
When seven is devided by three, the quotient is two and the remainder is one. 		七割る三は二、余り一


Comments:
  • () means no pronunciation.

  • We use equally 'si' and 'yon' for the number 4. But 'si' means death in Japanese. I prefer 'yon' for 4.

The Showa era began in 1926.
The Heisei era began on January 8, 1989.
昭和元年(しょうわがんねん)＝昭和一年=1926
平成元年(へいせいがんねん)＝平成一年=1989

You can calculate your birth year in the Showa to minus 1925 before January 8, 1989.
You can calculate your birth year in the Heisei to minus 1988 after January 8, 1989.
