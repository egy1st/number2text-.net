Part 3: How to count in Russian - Russian language tutorial and phrasebook

Submitted by admin on Fri, 08/14/2009 - 18:31

  

After the alphabet, counting is a very important thing to know. If you know the numbers, you can ask people about prices, read and understand the schedules and so on. You can listen to how the numbers sound below if you click on QuickTime or WinMedia "listen" link. I recorded them myself, but hopefully you enjoy it :)
